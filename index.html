NASA's Webb and Galactic Collisions

Nasa's James Webb Space Telescope has provided stunning new insights into galactic collisions.

These collisions can trigger star formation and influence the structure of galaxies.

Webb's observations allow scientists to study the intricate details of these cosmic events.

Understanding galactic collisions helps us learn about the evolution of galaxies over billions of years.

The data collected from Webb is crucial for revealing the processes that shape the universe.
